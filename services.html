Detailed Service Breakdown

Our personal support service is built for individuals who want their Apple devices to “just work.” We help with the frustrating bits — and show you a few simple tips that save time every day.

  • Troubleshooting for Mac, iPhone, and iPad — fast remote help and optional onsite visits.
  • Performance tuning & system cleanup to make older devices feel fresh again.
  • Secure backups (Time Machine, iCloud) and data migration to a new device.
  • Apple ID and iCloud troubleshooting — syncing, family sharing, and storage management.
  • One-on-one walkthroughs and training so you get more done with less fuss.

We help small teams run reliably — from onboarding new hires to securing shared drives. Our approach balances security and usability so your people can focus on work.

  • Device provisioning and management (MDM guidance and setup for Apple fleets).
  • Email, calendar and domain configuration (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace).
  • Secure Wi-Fi, VPN advice, and local network setup for stable connectivity.
  • Printer and file-sharing workflows — reduce friction for everyday tasks.
  • Policy recommendations: backups, access control, and incident response basics.

Our managed plans give you ongoing protection and predictable budgeting. We proactively maintain systems so issues are caught early — often before you notice them.

  • Regular maintenance windows and proactive health checks.
  • Patch management and security updates for macOS and key apps.
  • Backup monitoring and recovery testing to protect critical data.
  • Priority support for plan members, with remote troubleshooting included.
  • Quarterly reviews and recommendations to keep systems aligned with business goals.

Move your team to reliable cloud tools without the guesswork. We focus on secure, practical setups that make collaboration simple and backups reliable.

  • Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace setup, migration, and management.
  • iCloud for Business guidance and team sharing strategies.
  • Cloud backup planning and permission controls for safe sharing.
  • Integrations and automation to reduce manual tasks.
